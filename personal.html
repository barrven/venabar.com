Bio

Programming is hard. But building something that's beautiful and useful is pretty awesome, and as the saying goes, "no pain, no gain".

I grew up as a musician and completed a Bachelor's degree in music performance from the University of Toronto. After graduating, I decided that I wanted to investigate my other skills and potential outside of music, so I worked as an office clerk for a few years. This is where I first discovered that spreadsheets are pretty cool! Fast forward a few years and here I am about to graduate from George Brown College with an advanced diploma in Computer Programming.

I enjoy working with people to solve problems and practice solutions. I've worked as an English tutor, and a programming tutor, and I continue to teach music on the weekends.
