Capstone Project

Project Summary and Vision

This app will provide a focused, streamlined, and yet flexible connection tool for new, introverted, or online-based students. The primary goal is to encourage connection and collaboration among fellow students specifically at the same college in a way that is more effective than the tools which currently exist.

  • Focused
    • Users must belong to the same college - profile requires college email address.
    • Users provide profile information about their campus, program, semester, section, and classes - this enables groups formed to be focused at any of these levels
    • Can be used by teachers for class-specific group projects
  • Streamlined
    • Once profile information is filled out, users can ideally create or join a well-matched group within a few clicks
  • Flexible
    • Allows for creation of different kinds of groups, pulling from pool of colleagues with varying levels of specificity

Once groups are formed, the app aims to provide methods to quickly share contact information, and connect through the collaboration channels of the group’s choice.

It also aims to provide the additional functionality of feedback from user to user in terms their skills and personalities with the aim of improving suggested matches for future groups and helping users learn more about themselves
